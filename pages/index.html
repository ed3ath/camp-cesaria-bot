Privacy Policy for CWS Dev Bot

At CWS Dev Bot, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide to us when using our messenger bot.

Information Collection

When you use CWS Dev Bot, we collect the following personal information from you:

  • First name
  • Last name
  • Gender

We collect this information in order to address you properly and to provide you with personalized ads (if you choose to receive them). We do not collect any other personal information, such as email address, phone number, or address.

Information Use

We use the personal information collected from you to provide you with personalized messages and ads (if you choose to receive them) through our messenger bot. We do not share this information with third parties for any other purposes.

Information Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or use. We use industry-standard security measures, such as encryption and secure servers, to protect your personal information. However, no security measures are 100% effective and we cannot guarantee the security of your information.

Your Choices

You have the option to opt-in to receiving personalized ads from CWS Dev Bot. If you choose to opt-in, we will use your personal information to provide you with ads that we think you may be interested in. If you choose to opt-out, we will not use your personal information to provide you with ads.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time by posting a new version on our website. You should review this policy periodically to stay informed of any changes.
